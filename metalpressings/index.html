Welcome to Metal Pressings & Engg. Works

Metal Pressings & Engg. Works is a professionally managed Proprietorship firm in the Business of manufacturing and supply of Sheet Metal Components, Three Point Linkage parts and Fabricated components to O.E.Ms.

The Company was started in the year 1981 By Late Shri. O.P.Arora with an objective of Supplying sheet metal and fabricated components to the major manufacturers in Automobile, Tractor & Electrical industry. We are an IATF Certified Company.

We are equipped with full range of machinery, skilled manpower and In-House Tool room facility to manufacture wide range of sheet metal and Three Point Linkage Parts with ably supported quality assurance cell and an excellent core group of management, where every individual is committed to meet the customers most demanding requirements.

Infrastructure & Resource
Infrastructure & Resource

We have covered area of 30000 Sq. Feet. Industry at Northern India complex Sec-5 F.B.D & 13600 Sq. feet Industry at Saroorpur equipped with all primary facilities, work force & modern machines and tools.

Press shop is equipped with 35 presses with capacity range of 5 ton to 350 tons. We manufacture sheet metal & fabricated components from sheet thickness of 0.5 mm to 30 mm.

We are manufacturing tubular and pipe bending component on CNC PIPE BENDING Machine and Machined Fabricated parts on VMC & Other Allied converternal Milling & Lathes.
