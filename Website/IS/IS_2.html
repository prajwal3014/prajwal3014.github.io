Why to learn Information Security

Greetings of the day everyone, this is Prajwal Sharma. I hope you all liked my previous blog. So, in this blog, I am going to throw some light on the topic “Why to learn Information Security”.

Let me ask some questions :
• Why you all keep your mobile Phones locked?
• Why people keep their bank details secret?
• Why people didn’t show their property papers?

What is the answer of all these questions?
In one sentence I will give the answer => Because Security of all the Confidential data or private Information is very important. Suppose you are sleeping and suddenly you get calls from your friends, you answered the call and they started fighting with you on the call and blocks you. You didn’t understand what just happened. Then you open your mobile and you can see that someone is texting inappropriate messages to your friends.

Why this happened?
This happens because you didn’t take care about your information security like protecting your passwords or you just leave your social media account on someone’s phone.

Suppose you are going to start a new business or new company. At starting point, nobody knows you so your data is secured as no one want your data. But later on, when your company boost up its economy and company come with the eye of public. Now your company’s data are in threat as many hackers are looking to breach into your system and stole the data or money. By reading these examples you can think about the importance of information security. In brief, if you didn’t want to get leaked your private information you have to learn this subject.

I also want to tell you about some main roles in companies :
• CISO [Chief Information Security Officer]
• Information Security Architect
• Information Security Consultant
• Information Security Specialist
• Information Security Analyst
• Information Security Auditor
• Security Software Developer
• Penetration Tester/ Ethical Hacker
• Vulnerability Assessor

So, this is all about the Why to learn Information Security. Thank you to be so patient.
