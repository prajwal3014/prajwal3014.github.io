Introduction & History

Greetings of the day everyone, this is Prajwal Sharma and I have done certifications in information security and ethical hacking. So, in this series of blogs, I am going to give you a full tutorial on information security.

When we start learning anything, first we need is the meaning or definition of the subject. So, what is information security?
In simple language, the safety and protection of our confidential data or private information from unauthorized access is called information security.
Information can be physical or digital one. It can be anything like your biometrics, your bank account details, your social media account password.
In today’s world, we all listen some cases of fraud like unwanted deduction of money from bank accounts. Why this happens?
Because many people are not aware about the security of their confidential data like card no., CVV, OTP, etc. so we have to be alert because anyone can steal our data and misuse it.

Now coming to the history here are some information I get :
• Talking about late 1960’s, many industries started taking care of their information because anyone with full knowledge of computer can easily access the confidential data.
• During the1970’s, there was no internet so we can’t even imagine about computer networking, so large organization like government started connecting computers via telephone lines. So, people started penetrating into phone lines so that they can steal data and hence they become the first group of hackers.
• Let me tell you about an interesting case held during 1980’s, a small group of teenagers from Milwaukee, known as “the 414s” hacked 60 military and corporate computer systems and stole over $70 million from U.S. banks. After this case, the government started taking the fact information security seriously.

• Jumping to 2010’s, the technology is so improved and very advanced. So, there are new technologies like firewalls, antivirus software, etc. which help many organizations to take the care of their information security.
• Nowadays, everyone is taking the security measures at the topmost level.

So, this is all about the introduction and history of information security. Thank you to be so patient. I hope you gained a brief knowledge about information security.
