Shore Temple

Greetings of the day everyone, this is Khushi and I am pursuing Diploma in Architectural Assistantship. I hope you liked my previous blog. So, in this blog, We talk about Shore Temple.

Location – Mahabalipuram, Tamil Nadu

Architecture – Dravidian architecture

Built In – (700 – 728)

Built by – Narsimhavarman, pallava dynasty


SHORE TEMPLE, MAHABALIPURAM

o The shore temple is the five - storied structural Hindu temple.
o It is the earliest important structural temple in southern India.

o Its pyramidal structure is 60 ft high and 50 ft square platforms.

o There is a small temple in front and which was the original porch.

o It is made of finely cut local granite.

o The temple is a combination of three shrines.

o The main shrine dedicated to Shiva as is the smaller second shrine.

o The smaller third shrine, between the two is dedicated to a reclining vishnu and may have had water channel into the temple, entering the vishnu shrine.

o The two shiva shrines are orthogonal in configuration.

o The entrance is through the barrel vault gopuram.

o The two Sikhara have a pyramidal outline, each individual tier is distinct, with overhanging eaves that cast dark shadow.

o The outer wall of the shrine to vishnu and the inner side of the wall are extensively sculptured and topped by large sculptures of nandi.

o The temple outer wall is divided by plastes into bays, the lower part being carved into a series of rearing lions.


The shore Temple at mahabalipuram datable to late 7th century is specially known because of its location on the sea-shore. This temple through very similar stylistically to the Dharmaraja ratha differs from it in an important respect that it is a structural temple not a rock- cut one. It is about 3 to 4 times the size of the dharmaraja ratha and is made a tripple structure by adding a shrine at the back and slightly juttingout in the front. There are two Spier, much loftier than in the case of the previous temple, and the higher spires has more storey than the dharmaraja ratha and the pinnacle is higher and pointed. It is much more complicated enlarge and enriched.
