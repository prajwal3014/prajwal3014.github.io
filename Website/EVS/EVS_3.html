Problems we are dealing with and we could deal in near future

Greetings of the day, I hope you all are in good health. I am Khwaish Verma and I welcome you in this environmental studies series. In today’s blog, we will study about “Problems we are facing in daily life or we could face in near future”.

Since now we learned what is environment, what is environmental studies, its importance and its goals too.

Before getting into the topic, let’s study how to find out problems that we could deal with. The process is not so complicated. But the data requirement can be in high amount.

Steps to find out the problem are:-
1. SCIENTIFIC ASSESSMENT – First step is to collect the data of the environmental changes occurring from 50-60 years and study that data and focus on the changes in each year and each month. Now after complete analysis of the final report, the problem or the flaw has been found.
2. RISK ANALYSIS – According to the collected data, the future risk of the problem is calculated which helps in making a plan to protect us from the problem.
3. PUBLIC AWARENESS AND INVOLVEMENT – After calculating the risk level of the problem, all the person got to know about that specific problem, its causes, and its solution too to decrease the risk of future problem.
4. POLITICAL ACTION - Concerned authorities are also provided with information of the problem and are told to take action in preference of better environment. But the final decision in preference of the political, social and economical feasibility.
5. EVALUATION – After all these steps, data is collected again in order to check whether the problem is really decreasing or not.

PROBLEMS WE ARE REALLY DEALING WITH RIGHT NOW
• Disposal of waste material : Waste material is made by us (humans) in a very large quantity each day and its disposal is huge problem till now. These can contain toxic material which can cause pollution on burning, death to an animal and most of it can’t be recycled easily.
• Depletion of natural resources : Natural resources became a part of our life. Our life can’t be imagined without them. So we are using them without thinking now they are coming to an end. So new and alternate resources are required which must be easily available.
• Deforestation : We humans get everything from the trees, plants and crops that we grow but because of our greed of getting more, we remove them and don’t care at all.
• Public implementation : This is one of the major problem that people should change asap. Many people are concerned about the environmental issues we are facing but they do nothing in order to change it.

These are some major problems we are facing and cause a great harm to human society in near future.

It’s a kind request of mine please involve in your surrounding betterment to help our and future generation. Thank you for paying attention.
