Solution to Those problems

Greeting of the day, KHWAISH VERMA this side. I hope you all are staying safe. Welcome back to our course of environmental studies, where we study about the importance, working and problems we created in our environment and how to solve it. The main aim of the course is to create awareness or change the way of people’s thinking towards our environment.

So in our previous blog we studied about the various problems that we are currently dealing with and also got to know how they can cause high consequences to us. Let’s do a quick recap.

SOME PROBLEMS ARE:-
1. HIGH AMOUNT OF DEFORESTATION FOR OUR OWN GREED.
2. LACK OF PUBLIC AWARNESS AND INVOLVEMENT.
3. DISPOSAL OF WASTE PRODUCED.
4. DEPLETION OF NATURAL RESOURCES AND HARSH EFFECTS OF ITS OVERUSE.
5. NO FOOD EQUALITY FOR ALL.
6. RAPID INCRESE IN POPULATION.

Since we are dealing with some major problems, we need solutions that must be viable, and don’t harm much to our environment. SOLUTIONS TO THESE PROBLEMS:-
1. First of all, we should take care of our highly increase in population. More population means more demand which finally results in depletion of resources.
2. Public participation is the most important thing because most of our people are educated enough that they know what they have to do in order to protect our environment but they don’t care about that things.
3. We humans need to start using the alternate use of energy like biofuels, sunlight , wind energy instead of current sources(fossil fuels) because their remainder cause a huge harm to our environment.
4. We should stop wasting the things. Instead of that we should we can reduce, reuse, recycle the things that are provided by the nature free of cost.

We, individually and collectively can do much more to protect and restore our environment. Being, aware of the problems we face is the first step towards finding their solutions.
