Introduction & History

Greetings of the day everyone, Khwaish Verma this side. In this blog series we are going to study about environment, its working, its necessity, and almost everything about environment.

WHAT IS ENVIRONMENT?
ENVIRONMENT is defined as the natural world around us. It is not only the trees but all the living organisms present in our surrounding are considered as part of environment. Just look around yourself and realize for a second how beautiful our environment is. Do you find this pleasant? Now think of your childhood environment, do you find it more attractive, calm than our today one? So, this is the only reason to get necessary environmental education.

WHAT ARE ENVIRONMENTAL STUDIES?
Environment studies is the study of environment and our existence in it. It is basically nature and technology going hand in hand without hearting each other causing harm to us. Environmental study is not a professional degree, it is a general awareness that everyone should know and implement too. Many people know about environment, how to protect it but don’t do anything about this. As a concerned student, it’s a generous request please protect our environment for better quality of life.

PROBLEMS WE ARE DEALING WITH :
1. Rapid increase in human population
2. Depletion of natural resources
3. Disposal of waste material
4. Food and water shortage
5. Deforestation and clearing of irrigated land

These are some problems we all are dealing with and can cause a high damage to human society in near future.

That’s all for this blog, thank you for paying attention to this. I hope we can make our EARTH a better Place to live by taking short steps. In next blog, we will study about some solutions, ways to find problem that can occur in future and a rise to new technology.
