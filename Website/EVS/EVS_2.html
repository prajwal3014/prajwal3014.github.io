Need and Importance of environmental studies

Greetings of the day, welcome back to our blog. I hope you all are doing good. So in our previous blog, we learnt about what is environment, how it is different from what we think and also learnt what is environmental studies? Hope you all loved the previous blog.

Now today we will get over the topic “Need and Importance of environmental studies”. So let’s start =>

ENVIRONMENTAL EDUCATION is the consistent study and experience about the working and functioning of environment and developing skills to make our environment a better living place.

Since our environment got disturbed by the various human activities. So this had became a key concern for all of us to learn more about our environment and learn how to keep it safe.

IMPORTANCE :-
1. Everybody in our country is not getting same quality and quantity of food because of using poor quality of seeds of using many fertilizers. So, environmental education defines equal standard of food to all.
2. It also focuses people awareness towards rapid increase in human population and its side-effects.
3. Aware population towards the depletion of natural resources and inspire people to use alternate sources of energy.

GOALS OF ENVIRONMENTAL STUDIES :-
1. KNOWLEGE – Environment Studies provide opportunities to gain knowledge and build a healthy relationship with our environment.
2. AWARENESS – It spread awareness among the society be facing in near future.
3. SKILLS – It helps to develop skills in order to get better living conditions for our future generation.
4. ALTITUDE – It change the way of thinking of individual towards our environment. It helps to make a large difference in thinking of our future generation.
5. PARTICIPATION – Change in our environment can’t be made by one person only. Participation of everyone is necessary.

Our goal is to make our earth a better place to live. This is only possible by taking small changes everyone to make a great difference. Join in our mission.
